A Postmodern Visual Approach to Data Mining:

Proposed Timeline

Context:

I’m anticipating three data clusters and three to six collages. In weeks I’m predicting ten hours per collage, though it could take more or less depending on style; the physical collage and it’s digitized counterpart will be made semi-simultaneously. Early in the semester I’m anticipating three to four hours per week spent on the website and four to six hours spent on research and later in the semester seven to ten hours spent on the website or related skills. The image library will be amassed throughout the semester.
