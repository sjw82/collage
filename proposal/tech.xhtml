Data Management

I will be conducting data mining, i.e. using a computer to identify patterns and relationships within data sets, on the GenderWatch database which focuses on men’s and women’s gender roles but also includes LGBTQ+ studies, family studies, and women’s studies. I will cluster documents into subtopics and mine and collage them separately; for example I may have an Avant Garde men’s gender roles panel, a Dadaist motherhood panel, and a Postmodern transgender rights panel.

Web Development and Interactivity

All of the art styles that I am encompassing in this project feature an element of subjectivity; the viewer alters the artwork. To keep with that motif, the collage will be available online in a clickable format that allows the viewer to interact with the independent parts of the artwork, to take control of their experience of the piece.
